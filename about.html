With over 25 years of culinary excellence, our head chef is a master of crafting unforgettable meals. Inspired by traditional Indian cuisine and global flavors, his creations bring a story to each plate.

Our Story

Founded in 2010, My Restaurant began as a dream to bring a soulful dining experience to our community. With just 5 tables and a vision, we’ve grown into one of the city's top-rated destinations for food lovers.

From farm-fresh ingredients to heartwarming service, everything we do is rooted in love and tradition.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To serve delicious, affordable food that guests will want to return to week after week.

Our Vision

To be recognized as the region's most loved restaurant by staying true to our values and customers.
